Moong sprouts salad Recipe

Description

Learn how to make green moong sprouts salad with how to make sprouts at home. With spices to match Indian taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Whole moong sprouts
  • ¼ cup Chopped onion
  • ¼ cup Chopped tomato
  • ¼ cup Grated carrot
  • ¼ cup Grated radish
  • 1 Lemon's juice 2 tsp
  • ½ teaspoon Chaat masala
  • 1 tablespoon Cilantro chopped
  • Salt & pepper - As needed

Steps

  1. Just toss everything in a bowl. Enjoy immediately. Add salt carefully as chat masala already has salt in it. Such easy salad right?
